About Us
 

When you have a persistent pain, or a rash or cold that's lingered, you can only go so far diagnosing yourself on the Internet, and then you need to see a physician. You likely go back to the Internet or your insurance company to find one. Though you can read and compare various physicians' credentials, what you are really looking for is someone who will listen and pay attention to you. You want a doctor that is professional and personable, caring and compassionate. I found one recently – Dr. Ayisha Gani at North side Medical specialist in Canton.


Board-certified in internal medicine, Dr.Gani has been practicing medicine for 13 years, and it's very gratifying to be able to help patients feel better.''

She looks me in the eye, and I can see she cares. She wants me to feel better. And after she's ruled out all the life-threatening illnesses that I feared I might have (fear likely fueled by my Internet research), I do feel better. My toes will not fall off, I am not going to lose a lung or have to take any medications that will produce those dreaded side effects they are compelled to disclose on television. I do however, need to drink more water. Who hasn't heard that before?


I leave with a spring in my step, grateful for her extensive expertise, grateful that she loves practicing medicine and especially grateful that she takes the time to do it right.
